Need an ID? Check out the types of ID below

  • Newark Municipal ID (NMID) is a photo identification card and proof of residency for Newark residents, which enable you to access city government services, schools, libraries, and also open a bank account. The NMID card is a broadly accepted, Official form of identification within the City of Newark. The card also can be used for discounts at participating health and wellness centers, prescription programs, movie theaters, and more.
  • Documents to apply include: proof of identification (ex. birth certification, social security card, school enrollment ) and proof of residency (ex. utility bill).
  • The residency proof (PSEG bill, lease orbank statement) can be in the adult parents name they reside with.
  • You can apply regardless of immigration status.
  • The city id can be used for libraries, some banks and other city perks - it is also very helpful for those who are undocumented.
  • Application Fees: $7 for youth up to age 19; $15 for Adults over 19. Financial hardship waivers available
